Interactive Chart And Map That Summarize Tree Cover Loss

Data From Global Forest Watch Portal
2017/2021

From 2017 to 2021, Denizli lost 6.08kha of tree cover, equivalent to a 7.3% decrease in tree cover since 2010.100% of tree cover loss occurred within natural forest. The total loss within natural forest was equivalent to 1.60Mt of CO₂e emissions. The top 3 regions were responsible for 54% of all tree cover loss between 2017 and 2021. Acıpayam had the most tree cover loss at 1.37kha compared to an average of 320ha. Lost 6.08kha of relative tree cover, equivalent to a 100% decrease since 2000 and 2.6% of the global total.
